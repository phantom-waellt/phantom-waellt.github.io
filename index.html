Understanding Phantom Wallet:

Phantom is a non-custodial wallet designed specifically for the Solana blockchain, known for its high throughput and low transaction costs. The wallet serves as a secure bridge between users and the decentralized ecosystem, allowing them to manage their digital assets and interact with various decentralized applications seamlessly. One of its distinguishing features is its user-friendly interface, which simplifies the onboarding process for both novice and experienced users.
